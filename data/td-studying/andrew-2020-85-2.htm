Introduction

Introduction: The reason why there is no Moore's Law in Optics:

There are only four ways for controlling propagation of light with transparent material

  1. Shape
  2. refractive index
  3. birefringence
  4. the orientation of optical anisotropy

In contrast, there are many ways of generating light.
